When analyzing logs:

1. Determine log size and scope
2. Identify the log format and structure
3. Identify identifiers and correlation mechanisms
4. Correlate related events
5. Identify warnings, errors, and anomalies
6. Identify significant business or system events
7. Produce a concise summary
